- To change the Job Categories Manually 
Online : Go to https://findjobsinfinland.fi/jobs_table.html and copy the id of the job and then go to https://github.com/prastikaac/jobs/actions/workflows/change_category.yml  and click on run workflow and paste the id of the job and select the new category
Offline : Run python scraper/offline_manual_category_changer.py and then go to scraper\offline_manual_category_changer.html to update
