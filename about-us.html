About Us

findjobsinfinland.fi is a job platform dedicated to helping individuals find employment opportunities in Finland. Whether you are seeking full-time employment, part-time roles, or seasonal work in Finland, our platform connects you with the latest opportunities across sectors such as hospitality, healthcare, IT, logistics, construction, and more.

Many job seekers explore options including jobs in Finland for foreigners, English-speaking positions, and roles with visa sponsorship. Our website is designed to meet these needs by listing jobs suitable for various skill levels, language abilities, and professional backgrounds.

We recognize that individuals often ask how to get a job in Finland, where to apply, and whether it is possible to work in Finland using only English. Our goal is to answer these questions by providing direct access to reliable job listings tailored to different profiles.

Whether you are searching for jobs in Helsinki, Oulu, Lapland, or other regions, we aim to support your employment journey in Finland with clarity and confidence. We are here to assist your journey toward employment in Finland. Stay tuned with us!

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers in Finland. We are committed to supporting fair, transparent, and accessible recruitment by empowering individuals with the tools and resources they need to build a better future.

What We Offer

  • A continuously updated list of job vacancies from companies across Finland
  • Filters by job category, location, and employment type to make searching easy
  • Simple and direct application process for users
  • Resources to help users improve their employability and job readiness

Our Users

We proudly support a diverse community of users including:

  • Newcomers to Finland looking for their first job
  • International students seeking part-time work
  • Professionals aiming to build or continue their careers
  • Finnish employers looking to connect with motivated job seekers

Why Choose Us

findjobsinfinland.fi is more than a job board. We are built with the goal of making employment more inclusive, especially for those who face barriers in the Finnish job market. We value diversity, equal opportunity, and empowerment through employment.
